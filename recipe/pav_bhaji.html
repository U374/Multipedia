PAV BHAJI

Pav Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potatoes, peas, carrots, capsicum)
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Pav (bread rolls) for serving

Instructions:

  1. Boil the mixed vegetables until soft. Mash them coarsely and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat butter. Add chopped onions, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and bell peppers. Cook until tomatoes are soft and mushy.
  4. Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add the mashed vegetables to the pan. Mix thoroughly and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Toast pav with butter on a griddle until golden brown.
  8. Serve hot pav bhaji with toasted pav, chopped onions, and a lemon wedge.
