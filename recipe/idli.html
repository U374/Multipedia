Idli

Idli Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups idli rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak the idli rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind the urad dal and fenugreek seeds to a smooth batter. Similarly, grind the idli rice to a slightly coarse batter.
  3. Mix both batters together, add salt, and let it ferment overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  4. Grease the idli molds with oil and pour the batter into each mold.
  5. Steam the idlis for about 10-12 minutes until they are cooked through.
  6. Allow the idlis to cool for a few minutes before removing them from the molds.
  7. Serve hot idlis with coconut chutney and sambar.

Coconut Chutney Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1/2 cup roasted chana dal
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Grind grated coconut, green chilies, roasted chana dal, tamarind paste, and salt into a smooth chutney.
  2. Temper mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves, and red chilies in hot oil. Add the tempering to the chutney.
  3. Coconut chutney is ready to be served with idlis.

Sambar Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup toor dal
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, potato, drumstick)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup tamarind extract
  • Sambar masala powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida
  • Curry leaves
  • Coriander leaves for garnishing
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Pressure cook toor dal until soft. Mash it and keep aside.
  2. Cook mixed vegetables with tamarind extract, sambar masala powder, salt, and water until vegetables are tender.
  3. Add mashed toor dal to the cooked vegetables and mix well.
  4. Prepare a tempering of mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Add it to the sambar.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves. Serve hot sambar with idlis.
