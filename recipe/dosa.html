DOSA

Delicious Dosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for soaking and grinding
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately.
  2. Soak the rice and fenugreek seeds together in water for about 4-6 hours.
  3. Soak the urad dal in water for about 4-6 hours.
  4. Grind the soaked rice and fenugreek seeds into a smooth batter.
  5. Grind the soaked urad dal into a smooth and fluffy batter.
  6. Mix both batters together, add salt, and allow the dosa batter to ferment overnight or for 8-12 hours.
  7. Heat a griddle or dosa pan, spread a ladleful of batter in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  8. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges of the dosa and cook until it becomes golden brown and crispy.
  9. Flip the dosa and cook the other side for a brief moment.
  10. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
