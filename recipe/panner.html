Butter Paneer Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cashews, soaked
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add cashews, pureed tomatoes, red chili powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add paneer cubes and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Stir in cream and simmer for an additional 2 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
