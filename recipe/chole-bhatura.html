Chole Bhature Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chickpeas (chole), soaked overnight
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste

For Bhature:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed for kneading
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Boil soaked chickpeas until tender, then set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, chopped onions, and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and chopped tomatoes, cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Stir in turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add boiled chickpeas, mix well, and simmer until the gravy thickens.
  6. For Bhature, mix all-purpose flour, yogurt, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Knead into a soft dough.
  7. Divide the dough into small balls, roll them out, and deep-fry in hot oil until golden brown.
  8. Serve Chole with hot Bhature.
