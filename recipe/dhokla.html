Dhokla

Dhokla Delight

A Taste of India

Discover the joy of making and savoring Dhokla, a delightful Indian treat!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 tablespoon Eno fruit salt
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine chickpea flour, yogurt, turmeric powder, and ginger-garlic paste. Mix well to form a smooth batter.
  2. Allow the batter to ferment for 4-6 hours or overnight.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and asafoetida. Let them splutter.
  4. Add this tempering to the fermented batter and mix well.
  5. Grease a steaming dish and add Eno fruit salt to the batter. Mix gently and pour the batter into the dish.
  6. Steam the batter for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean from the center.
  7. Once done, let it cool and then cut into squares.
  8. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  9. Serve the dhokla pieces with mint chutney or your favorite sauce.

Enjoy your homemade Dhokla Delight!
